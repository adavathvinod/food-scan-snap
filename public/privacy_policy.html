1. Introduction

FoodyScan ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password when you create an account
  • Health Data: Weight, height, age, gender, dietary goals, and health conditions you choose to share
  • Food Images: Photos of meals you upload for nutrition analysis
  • Medical Reports: Lab reports or medical documents you upload (optional)
  • Chat History: Conversations with our AI assistant

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, scan history, time spent in app
  • Location Data: Approximate location (if you grant permission) for localized nutrition data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing nutrition analysis and calorie information
  • Generating personalized diet plans and health recommendations
  • Improving AI model accuracy and app features
  • Sending notifications about meal reminders (if enabled)
  • Analyzing app performance and user experience
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Google Gemini AI

Food images and chat messages are processed by Google's Gemini AI for image recognition, nutrition analysis, and conversational responses. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 CalorieNinjas API

Food names are sent to CalorieNinjas for detailed nutrition data. Their privacy policy: https://calorieninjas.com/privacy

4.3 OneSignal (Push Notifications)

Device tokens and notification preferences are managed by OneSignal. Privacy policy: https://onesignal.com/privacy_policy

4.4 Supabase (Database & Authentication)

User data is stored on Supabase infrastructure. Privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication with password hashing
  • Row-level security policies on database
  • Regular security audits

However: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Scan History: Retained until you delete or close your account
  • Medical Reports: Stored until manually deleted
  • Chat History: Retained for 90 days
  • Account Data: Deleted within 30 days of account closure request

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (account deletion)
  • Portability: Export your data in JSON format
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and analytics

8. Children's Privacy

FoodyScan is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage for session management and preferences. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell data).

13. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

EU users have rights under GDPR including data access, rectification, erasure, and portability. Our legal basis for processing is consent and legitimate interest.
